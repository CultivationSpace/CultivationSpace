Frequently Asked Questions

How was Cultivation Space founded?

Cultivation Space was originally founded as Onion Space in 2015, a community space for the Tor Project, also known as Zwiebelfreunde e.V. Throughout the years, various other communities and individuals used the space, not limited to Tor activities only, hence in early 2022 we changed our name and identity to Cultivation Space, a hub where people cultivate technology for good (and also a nod to our fiscal host, Tech Cultivation).

Who is involved at Cultivation Space?

An assortment of organisations and illustrious individuals contribute and work from the space from time to time. Tech Cultivation is the legal entity for this space. Most people and projects work on open source, free software, internet freedom or journalism projects.

Why Wedding?

As you will have noticed in the history section, the ExRotaprint space has been our home from the beginning. The structure and environment allows us to maintain our open door policy and provide it to any and all. ExRotaprint also aligns with our values: As a non-profit (gGmbH), all projects within the complex are non-commercial and we want to keep it this way. You can easily reach the space by public transport (U9 Nauener Platz or S Wedding).

Can I become a member?

We currently don't do memberships. If you want to come to Cultivation Space get in touch with us via email This is an open space for exchange. Having said that, please familiarize yourself with our Code of Conduct applicable in our space (both physical and virtual). People who rely on the space and are vetted by the community receive an RFID key for accessing the space.

Do you have drinks and snacks?

We keep the fridge and snack bar stocked and have a small suggested donation amount for these. If you have any special requests please reach out so we can consider that in our next order. There is also coffee and tea to brew yourself

What amenities does the space provide?

We have 3 big tables for up to 5-6 people each in the big room, as well as 2 meeting rooms with TV and video conferencing hardware. There are also 2 telephone boxes (1 small and 1 big one). The unisex bathroom also offers a shower.

How much does it cost?

That depends. Cultivation Space is almost only financed by donations. We need less money than any other coworking space to run as we do not make a profit. If you get value out of the space, consider donating so we can keep paying the rent. If you are not sure how much to donate please get in touch.

I want to organize an event at the space, how does it work?

Please get in touch with us via [insert email here], and include details of the event as well as how much you can donate to cover the space costs. We can arrange scheduling afterwards.
